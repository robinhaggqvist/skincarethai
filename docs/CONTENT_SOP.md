# SkincareThai Content SOP

Thai-first routine for building new content on `skincarethai.com`.

## Weekly Rhythm

- Monday: choose 1 topic cluster and 1 search intent.
- Tuesday: collect source blogs, brand pages, product listings, and community references.
- Wednesday: draft the pillar article.
- Thursday: draft supporting pages and internal links.
- Friday: polish Thai wording, title, meta description, FAQ, and CTA.
- Weekend: update sitemap, internal links, and content notes.

## Content Buckets

- Problem-led: acne, dark spots, dull skin, oily skin, sensitive skin, dehydration.
- Product-led: cleanser, serum, sunscreen, moisturizer, essence, mask.
- Brand-led: one hub per brand with related product pages.
- Compare-led: brand vs brand or product vs product.
- Routine-led: morning, night, beginner, budget.
- Ingredient-led: niacinamide, centella, retinol, vitamin C, PDRN, hyaluronic acid.

## Page Formula

- Thai title that reads like a real search query.
- Short intro that says what the page solves.
- Clear sections with subheads.
- "เหมาะกับใคร"
- "จุดเด่น / ข้อควรระวัง"
- FAQ in natural Thai.
- 2 or more related internal links.

## Style Rules

- Use modern Thai, not translated marketing language.
- Keep sentences short and clear.
- Avoid repeating the same opening line on every page.
- Use English only when it is natural for product names or ingredients.

## Quality Check

- Does the title sound natural in Thai?
- Is the intro useful in 2 to 3 sentences?
- Is the vocabulary common in Thai beauty content?
- Are there repeated phrases that should be cut?
- Does the page point to related pages?
